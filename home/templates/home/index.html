About Court Love

Court Love offers tennis lessons that are fun and helpful. Our coaches are professionals with a long tennis career and have different areas of expertise. You can focus on your serve, net or baseline play, a specific play style and you can always come with suggestions. When you book a lesson, you will leave smiling knowing what to do when you are alone on the court next match!

How does it work

With us, you book only when it suits you. Check out our lessons and see what theme and date suits you. We offer both private and group lessons, for different levels and prices. You can book 1 place per person.

Register

You can register with us and create a profile. Fill in your level in your profile so your coach can prepare accordingly. If you want, your coach can also alter your level after a lesson.
